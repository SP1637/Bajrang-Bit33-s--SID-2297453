"Recipe Features" : Recipe Showcase: A Taste of Bajrang Bites

1.Butter Chicken (Murgh Makhani)

Marinate chicken in yogurt, spices, and salt.

Brown chicken in oil, set aside.

Sauté onions, add tomatoes, spices, and cook until oil separates.

Add chicken, simmer, then stir in cream.

Garnish with coriander, serve with naan or rice.

2.Palak Paneer (Spinach and Cottage Cheese Curry)

Blanch spinach, blend to paste

Fry paneer until golden, set aside.

Sauté onions, add tomatoes, spices, and cook.

Add spinach paste, simmer.

Add paneer, cook, garnish with cream.

3. Chole (Chickpea Curry)

Sauté onions, add spices, ginger-garlic paste

Add tomatoes, cook until oil separates.

Add chickpeas, simmer with water.

Season with garam masala, amchur, salt.

Garnish with coriander, serve hot.
